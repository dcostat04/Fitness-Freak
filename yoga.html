Yoga Poses 

Tree Pose


Tree pose strengthens the legs, and  improves your neuromuscular coordination .It helps with balance and endurance .

Mountain  Pose


Mountain Pose improves your posture and body awareness, strengthens your legs, and establishes good alignment. 

Warrior II Pose


Warrior II pose stretches your hips, groins and shoulders . Improves circulation and respiration.

Triangle Pose


Triangle Pose stretches legs, muscles around the knee, ankle joints, hips. It strengthens legs, knees, ankles, abdominals, obliques and back.
